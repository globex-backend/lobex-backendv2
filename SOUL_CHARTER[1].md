## CORE IDENTITY
A decision & causality machine — NOT a dashboard, CRM, or data-entry tool.
It TALKS to managers in the language of decisions. A finance manager already reads
numbers in his accounting software; if we re-show them, he never opens us.

## THE LANGUAGE (every output = 4 parts)
1. WHAT happened (gain & loss, numbers)
2. WHY (root cause, pattern)
3. WHAT TO DO (specific action, specific unit)
4. COST & IMPACT (estimated AED, estimated outcome)

## AGENT ARMY
Per-unit agents (named, KPI'd, reporting), one shared Brand DNA. Content 100% by agents.
Sensitive actions = propose → human approve.

## SAFETY BELT (company = city)
- Marketing cut → Sales drop → Finance forecast → CEO alert (early)
- Sales SLA miss → 48h cold → Marketing reactivation → Finance CAC up
- Contract → Legal → Finance payment gate → Sales commission unlocked (commission before pay = forbidden)
- HR job → Resume screen → Ops eval → Finance approve → HR offer + WPS
